# Deen360: The Zen-HUD Spiritual Companion

Deen360 is a premium, high-fidelity spiritual companion app designed to bring tranquility and structure to your daily worship. Built with a focus on "Zen-HUD" design principles, it provides a distraction-free, priority-driven roadmap for the modern Muslim.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🕋 Zen-HUD Dashboard
A minimalist, high-contrast spiritual roadmap that prioritizes what matters most. The Zen-HUD provides real-time guidance based on your local prayer times and spiritual goals.

### 📅 Advanced Salah Tracker (Evidence-Based)
A robust, historical prayer logging system aligned with authentic Sahih Hadith.
- **Flexible Rak‘ah Counts**: Record specific Sunnah (Before/After) and Nafl units.
- **Witr Emphasis**: Dedicated tracking for the highly emphasized Witr prayer.
- **Historical Calendar**: Visualize your spiritual consistency with a built-in progress tracker.
- **Authentic Guide**: Integrated reference for daily Salah structures.

### 📖 Reading Hub
Comprehensive access to Islamic knowledge in a premium reading environment.
- **Quran**: Navigate Surahs and Ayahs with a focus on clarity.
- **Hadith Collections**: Explore authentic narrations from primary sources.

### 🤲 Dua & Remembrance
- **Dua Hub**: Categorized supplications for every occasion.
- **Asma-Ul-Husna**: A beautiful, interactive gallery of the 99 Names of Allah.
- **Tasbih Counter**: Minimalist tools for Dhikr and remembrance.

### 🧭 Qibla & Tools
- **Precision Qibla Compass**: Modern sensor-driven orientation.
- **Prayer Schedules**: Accurate local timings with sunrise and night-period markers.

---

## 🎨 Design Philosophy
Deen360 utilizes a custom **Zen-HUD** design language:
- **Calm Palette**: Harmony between Emerald Green and Slate.
- **Micro-Animations**: Subtle feedback for every interaction.
- **Dynamic Themes**: Seamless transitions between light and dark modes.
